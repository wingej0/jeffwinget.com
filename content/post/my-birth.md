## Are there any stories about your birth that were told to you by your parents or other family members?

As far as I can tell, my birth was pretty uneventful.  I was a pretty small baby, and it was Grandma's second pregnancy.  Grandma and Grandpa were convinced I was a girl (my name was going to be Stephanie).  Grandma asked the doctor to check twice when he said I was a boy.

Jeffery => Feminine spelling, grandma had a Jeffery that was a girl in class.  They went through a lot to get my name changed.

Grandma met grandpa in church.  Grandpa asked her out because she had the shortest skirt in the chapel.
